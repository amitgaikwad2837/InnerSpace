Features

Everything you need to grow

InnerSpace combines five powerful tools in one private, offline-first app.

🧠

AI That Truly Listens

Emotionally intelligent AI adapts to your tone and needs — warm, direct, or motivational — never judging, always present.

🤝

35+ Specialist Helpers

Choose from a library of AI helpers across 9 life categories: career, relationships, wellness, creativity, finance, and more. Build your own with the custom helper creator.

🔒

100% On-Device Privacy

All data stays on your phone. No servers, no tracking, no cloud uploads. Journal entries and habits encrypted with AES-256-GCM and a hardware-backed key.

🌎

10 Languages

English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Hindi, Japanese, Mandarin, Arabic, and Italian — fully localised with automatic RTL layout support.

⚡

Local AI Option

Run Gemma, Phi, or Llama entirely on your device — no API key, no internet, no data ever leaves your phone. Works even in airplane mode.

🎯

Personalised from Day One

Choose your tone, pick your helpers, set your language. The onboarding flow remembers your preferences and shapes the entire experience around you.

New
🎤

Voice Input

Speak naturally instead of typing. Tap the microphone in any chat or journal screen — InnerSpace transcribes your words instantly using on-device speech recognition.

New
🏴

Goals & Habit Linking

Set meaningful goals with target dates and link them directly to your daily habits. See which habits are moving you toward each goal and stay on track.

New
✨

Smart Habit Suggestions

Ask your AI helper about building a new habit and a one-tap chip appears at the bottom of the chat to add it straight to your tracker — no copy-paste needed.

New
📞

Crisis Safety Net

A persistent “Need help now?” button on the home screen gives instant access to international crisis helplines and emergency contacts — always one tap away.

Privacy & Safety

Your data never leaves your device

InnerSpace is built around a simple promise: your personal growth is yours alone. Designed so there is nothing to leak, breach, or sell.

  • ✓
    Zero-server architectureAll data stored locally via AsyncStorage and expo-secure-store. No backend infrastructure exists.
  • ✓
    AES-256-GCM encryption at restJournal entries, habits, and conversations encrypted with a hardware-backed key stored in the secure enclave.
  • ✓
    Your API keys, your controlKeys are stored on-device in the OS secure enclave — never transmitted to InnerSpace or any third party.
  • ✓
    Dual-layer AI safety filterPre-send and post-response filtering. No medical, clinical, legal, or crisis advice is ever given.
  • ✓
    App lock — PIN or biometricLock the entire app behind your fingerprint, Face ID, or a custom PIN. Auto-locks when backgrounded.
  • ✓
    Full data export & deleteExport everything as an encrypted JSON backup. Uninstall and it is gone. Always.
