Privacy & Safety

Your data never leaves your device

InnerSpace is built around a simple promise: your personal growth is yours alone. Designed so there is nothing to leak, breach, or sell.

  • ✓
    Zero-server architectureAll data stored locally via AsyncStorage and expo-secure-store.
  • ✓
    AES-256-GCM encryption at restJournal entries and habits encrypted with a hardware-backed key.
  • ✓
    Your API keys, your controlKeys stored in the secure enclave — never transmitted to InnerSpace.
  • ✓
    Dual-layer AI safety filterPre-send and post-response filtering. No medical, clinical, or crisis advice.
  • ✓
    Full data export & deleteExport everything as JSON. Uninstall and it is gone. Always.
