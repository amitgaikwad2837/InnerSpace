Privacy Overview

InnerSpace is designed with privacy-first principles. Here's what you need to know:

  • No account required — You can use InnerSpace as a guest without creating an account
  • All data stays on your device — Your conversations, habits, journal entries, and decisions are stored locally
  • No servers or cloud storage — We don't have backend servers that store your personal information
  • No tracking — We don't track your behavior, analytics, or usage patterns
  • No ads — We don't serve advertisements or collect data for advertising
  • Encrypted at rest — Sensitive data like journal entries and habits are encrypted with AES-256-GCM

Data Collection

InnerSpace collects minimal data:

Data You Actively Create

  • Conversation messages (stored on your device)
  • Habit entries (stored on your device)
  • Journal entries (stored encrypted on your device)
  • Decision analysis notes (stored on your device)
  • User preferences (language, tone, theme)
  • Mood check-ins (stored on your device)

Data We Don't Collect

  • Your location
  • Your contacts or phone number
  • Your browsing history
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, GAID, etc.)
  • Behavioral analytics or session tracking

Data Storage & Encryption

On-Device Storage

All your personal data is stored on your device using React Native's AsyncStorage and expo-secure-store. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your phone unless you explicitly export it
  • If you uninstall the app, your data is removed
  • You have full control over what happens to your data

Encryption

Sensitive data (journal entries and habit logs) are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM:

  • Encryption key is stored in expo-secure-store (hardware-backed on iOS/Android)
  • Plaintext data is never readable without the decryption key
  • Encryption is transparent — you don't need to do anything

Third-Party Services

AI Providers

When you chat with InnerSpace, you may use AI from:

  • Google Gemini (default) — You provide your own API key
  • OpenAI — You provide your own API key
  • Anthropic Claude — You provide your own API key
  • Groq — You provide your own API key
  • Local models — Runs entirely on your device with no external calls
Important: When you use an external AI provider, your conversation messages are sent to that provider's servers according to their privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their policies:
  • Google Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Groq Terms

Your AI API Keys

If you provide an API key for Google, OpenAI, Claude, or Groq:

  • Keys are stored securely — Stored in expo-secure-store (encrypted)
  • Keys never leave your device — Except when you make AI API calls
  • You can delete your key anytime — Settings → AI Provider
  • We never access your keys — You are responsible for keeping them secret

Device Permissions

InnerSpace requests the following permissions:

  • Camera — Optional, for biometric authentication (Face ID / Touch ID)
  • Biometric/Face ID — Optional, for app lock authentication
  • Notifications — Optional, for habit reminders and daily check-ins
  • File access — Required for backup export and import

All permissions are optional. You can use InnerSpace without granting any permissions.

Backup & Export

You can export your data as a JSON file anytime:

  • Go to Settings → Backup & Restore
  • Tap "Export Data" to download your complete data
  • Share the file securely with yourself (email, cloud storage, etc.)
  • You can import the backup into InnerSpace on another device

You have full ownership and control over your exported data.

Children's Privacy

InnerSpace is intended for adults 18 and older. We do not:

  • Knowingly collect data from children under 18
  • Market InnerSpace to children
  • Allow children to create accounts

If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. When we do:

  • We'll update the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • Major changes will be announced in the app
  • Your continued use of InnerSpace means you accept the updated policy
